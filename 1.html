About Indie Icebreakers

Hey! I'm Arav! I'm 13 years old. This is my 4th product of the year, and this time I'm trying something new. Indie Icebreakers Is a community I'm building.

The Idea is pretty simple, it's to help cut out the akwardness while talking to new creators. You know that feeling, you dm or talk to some new/old and you feel akward about talking to them.

You don't know what to say what to say, or you just want to connect, and get advice yet you just don't know the right way. It's not easy. That's what You can see Aprilynne Mention in her Tweet. With Icebreaker, I'm trying to change that feeling, and just make it easy, by creating a community just for that.
